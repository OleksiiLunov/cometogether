# Product Vision Canvas

## Product
**Cometogether**

A platform designed to help musicians connect, form bands, collaborate on music projects, and organize rehearsals.

---

## Target Groups

### Primary Users
- Amateur musicians looking for bandmates  
- Semi-professional musicians seeking collaboration opportunities  
- Musicians who recently moved to a new city and want to connect with local players

### Secondary Users
- Existing bands looking for additional members  
- Music producers and session musicians  
- Rehearsal studios that want to promote and manage their rehearsal spaces

---

## User Needs / Problems

### Musicians
- Difficulty finding compatible bandmates  
- Lack of efficient tools to search musicians by instrument, genre, skill level, or location  
- Fragmented communication across different platforms  
- Difficulty finding and booking rehearsal spaces

### Rehearsal Studios
- Limited visibility among local musicians  
- Inefficient or manual booking processes  
- Lack of direct access to musician communities

---

## Product Description

Cometogether is a platform that enables musicians to:

- create structured musician profiles  
- find other musicians based on instrument, genre, skill level, and location  
- form bands and music projects  
- communicate with potential collaborators  
- discover and organize rehearsals  

In future versions, the platform may also allow rehearsal studios to list their spaces and manage rehearsal bookings.

---

## Value Proposition

Cometogether provides a dedicated ecosystem for musicians by offering:

- a platform specifically designed for music collaboration  
- structured musician profiles and advanced search capabilities  
- tools for forming bands and organizing music projects  
- direct communication between musicians  
- a future integrated system for discovering and booking rehearsal spaces  

---

## Alternatives / Competitors

Current alternatives used by musicians include:

- Facebook musician groups  
- Reddit communities  
- Local forums and classifieds  
- Personal networks and word of mouth  
- Specialized platforms such as BandMix or SoundBetter  

These alternatives often lack structured musician profiles, efficient search tools, and collaboration-focused workflows.

---

## Differentiation

Cometogether differentiates itself by providing:

- a platform focused exclusively on musicians  
- structured profiles tailored for musical collaboration  
- advanced discovery and matching capabilities  
- tools designed specifically for forming bands and music projects  
- potential integration with rehearsal space booking  

---

## Success Criteria

Early indicators of product success include:

- number of registered musicians  
- number of completed musician profiles  
- number of bands created on the platform  
- number of connections and messages between musicians  
- number of collaborations formed
