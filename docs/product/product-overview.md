# Product Overview

## Product Name
**Cometogether**

---

## 1. Vision Statement

Cometogether is a platform that helps musicians find bandmates, collaborate on music projects, and organize rehearsals in one dedicated ecosystem designed specifically for music creation.

---

## 2. Elevator Pitch

For musicians who want to find bandmates and collaborate on music projects,  
Cometogether is a platform for musician collaboration  
that helps users discover compatible musicians, form bands, and organize rehearsals.

Unlike general social networks or scattered online communities,  
Cometogether provides structured musician profiles, powerful discovery tools,  
and a dedicated environment designed specifically for music collaboration.

---

## 3. Product Vision Canvas

### Product
Cometogether is a platform designed to help musicians connect, form bands, collaborate on music projects, and organize rehearsals.

---

### Target Groups

#### Primary Users
- Amateur musicians looking for bandmates  
- Semi-professional musicians seeking collaboration opportunities  
- Musicians who recently moved to a new city and want to connect with local players  

#### Secondary Users
- Existing bands looking for additional members  
- Music producers and session musicians  
- Rehearsal studios (future phase)  

---

### User Needs / Problems

#### Musicians
- Difficulty finding compatible bandmates  
- Lack of efficient tools to search musicians  
- Fragmented communication across platforms  
- Difficulty finding rehearsal spaces  

#### Rehearsal Studios (Future Phase)
- Limited visibility among musicians  
- Manual booking processes  
- Lack of direct access to target audience  

---

### Product Description

Cometogether enables musicians to:

- create structured profiles  
- discover other musicians  
- form bands and music projects  
- communicate with collaborators  
- organize rehearsals  

Future versions may include rehearsal space listing and booking functionality.

---

### Value Proposition

- Built specifically for musicians  
- Advanced search and matching  
- Tools for collaboration and band formation  
- Direct communication  
- Future rehearsal booking integration  

---

### Alternatives / Competitors

- Facebook groups  
- Reddit communities  
- Local forums  
- Personal networks  
- Platforms like BandMix or SoundBetter  

---

### Differentiation

- Music-focused platform  
- Structured musician profiles  
- Collaboration-first approach  
- Matching and discovery features  
- Future rehearsal marketplace  

---

### Success Criteria

- Number of registered users  
- Profile completion rate  
- Number of bands created  
- User interactions (messages, connections)  
- Successful collaborations
