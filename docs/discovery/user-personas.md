# User Personas

## 1. Beginner Musician

### Profile
- Age: 18–25  
- Experience: Beginner  
- Instrument: Guitar / Vocals / Keyboard  
- Status: Student or early career  

### Goals
- Find people to play with  
- Gain experience playing in a band  
- Improve musical skills  

### Frustrations
- Doesn't know where to find other musicians  
- Feels intimidated by more experienced players  
- Hard to find people at the same skill level  

### Needs
- Simple onboarding  
- Ability to filter musicians by skill level  
- Friendly and low-pressure environment  

---

## 2. Intermediate Musician (Core Persona)

### Profile
- Age: 20–35  
- Experience: Intermediate  
- Instrument: Any  
- Status: Working or studying  

### Goals
- Find reliable bandmates  
- Play regularly (rehearsals, small gigs)  
- Possibly start a serious band  

### Frustrations
- Musicians are unreliable or not committed  
- Hard to find people with matching taste and goals  
- Time wasted on unstructured communication  

### Needs
- Advanced filtering (genre, skill, commitment level)  
- Clear musician profiles  
- Easy communication tools  
- Ability to form and manage bands  

---

## 3. Advanced / Semi-Professional Musician

### Profile
- Age: 25–45  
- Experience: Advanced  
- Instrument: Any  
- Status: Semi-professional or professional  

### Goals
- Find high-quality collaborators  
- Work on serious music projects  
- Participate in gigs or recordings  

### Frustrations
- Low signal-to-noise ratio on existing platforms  
- Difficult to assess skill level of other musicians  
- Lack of serious collaboration tools  

### Needs
- High-quality profiles (portfolio, recordings)  
- Precise search and filtering  
- Efficient communication  
- Reputation or credibility indicators  

---

## 4. Rehearsal Studio Owner (Future Persona)

### Profile
- Business owner or manager  
- Owns or operates rehearsal spaces  

### Goals
- Attract more musicians  
- Increase booking rate  
- Simplify booking management  

### Frustrations
- Empty time slots  
- Manual booking via phone or messages  
- Limited visibility  

### Needs
- Ability to list rehearsal spaces  
- Calendar and availability management  
- Booking system  
- Access to musician audience
